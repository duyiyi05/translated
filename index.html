Symbiotic Enterprise Localization System

Symbiotic Enterprise Localization System

by Duygu TAŞ

I. Philosophy

  • Humans and AI collaborate with clear role boundaries.
  • AI accelerates throughput; humans own nuance and accountability.
  • Quality is treated as a strategic business signal, not a final step.

II. Context Engineering

  • Onboarding packs codify audience, tone, product, and legal constraints.
  • EN→TR and EN→IT risk maps are set before first delivery cycle.
  • Context is versioned so AI prompts and reviewers stay aligned.

III. Human + AI Workflow

  • Intake → prep → AI-assisted drafting → human QA → delivery loop.
  • Glossary and TM governance enforce stable language memory.
  • Approval paths are explicit for legal and brand-critical decisions.

IV. Clarification Intelligence

  • EN→IT: formality and gender agreement escalated via blocker rules.
  • EN→TR: tone calibration and terminology consistency monitored weekly.
  • Question batching reduces decision fatigue while preserving SLA speed.

V. Enterprise Metrics

  • OTD, QA pass rate, rework trend, TAT, and risk exposure tracked monthly.
  • Signals tie linguistic quality directly to operational predictability.
  • Business review format enables stakeholder-grade reporting.

VI. 30-60-90 Plan

  • 30: baseline governance and measurement in place.
  • 60: measurable QA and rework gains through guided optimization.
  • 90: scaled throughput with controlled risk and executive confidence.
