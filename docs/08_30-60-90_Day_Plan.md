# 08 — 30-60-90 Day Plan

## Vision
Build a localization engine that is faster, safer, and more context-aware each month—without sacrificing linguistic quality.

## Day 0–30: Foundation and baselines

### Goals
- Complete onboarding context pack for both locales.
- Establish glossary/TM governance and approval SLA tracking.
- Launch KPI dashboard with first baseline report.

### Targets
- 100% projects tagged with style mode.
- 95% of blocker clarifications answered within 24h.
- Baseline QA pass rate captured for TR and IT.

## Day 31–60: Stabilize and optimize

### Goals
- Reduce avoidable rework through stronger terminology controls.
- Improve confidence routing for AI-assisted outputs.
- Standardize review checklists by content stream.

### Targets
- +5 points QA pass rate vs baseline.
- -15% major terminology errors.
- Clarifications per 1k words reduced by 10% via better pre-briefing.

## Day 61–90: Scale with control

### Goals
- Expand throughput while keeping risk indicators stable.
- Introduce targeted experiments on high-volume content.
- Build executive confidence with predictable monthly reviews.

### Targets
- OTD ≥ 96% sustained over rolling 30 days.
- Average TAT reduced by 12% from baseline.
- Open high-impact risks reduced by 25%.

## Operating commitments
- Human reviewers retain final authority on high-risk content.
- AI behavior is tuned with explicit context, not blind automation.
- Governance artifacts remain lightweight but enforceable.
