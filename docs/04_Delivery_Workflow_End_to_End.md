# 04 — Delivery Workflow End-to-End

## Workflow overview
Intake → Preparation → AI-assisted production → Human QA → Delivery → Feedback loop.

## 1) Intake
- Confirm source scope, deadlines, channels, and style mode.
- Validate context pack availability.
- Flag regulatory/legal content.

Exit criteria:
- Scope locked.
- Stakeholders assigned.
- Risks pre-registered.

## 2) Preparation
- Pre-translation analysis (repetitions, fuzzy matches, net-new words).
- Retrieve relevant glossary/TM slices.
- Configure AI assistance with style + terminology constraints.

Exit criteria:
- Asset bundle frozen for batch.
- Clarification queue initialized.

## 3) AI-assisted production
- AI generates first-pass proposals with context memory.
- Linguists post-edit with focus on meaning, tone, and term fidelity.
- Low-confidence segments auto-routed for deeper review.

Exit criteria:
- Complete draft with confidence tags.

## 4) Human QA
- Terminology checks.
- Consistency checks across related assets.
- Severity-based issue tagging and correction.

Exit criteria:
- No open critical issues.
- Pass threshold met per locale.

## 5) Delivery
- Package localized files + change notes.
- Send delivery report: on-time status, quality summary, known caveats.

Exit criteria:
- Client acknowledgment logged.

## 6) Feedback loop
- Capture client comments and internal QA patterns.
- Update glossary, TM weights, and clarification playbook.
- Feed issues into monthly business review.

Exit criteria:
- Improvement actions assigned with owners and due dates.

## SLA guardrails
- Clarification response SLA: < 24h for blockers.
- Critical fix turnaround: same business day.
- Weekly quality trend review across EN→TR and EN→IT.
