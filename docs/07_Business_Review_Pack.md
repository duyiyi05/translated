# 07 — Business Review Pack (Monthly)

## Objective
Provide an executive-ready monthly view of performance, risks, and improvement outcomes for EN→TR and EN→IT.

## Template agenda (60 minutes)
1. KPI snapshot (10 min)
2. Quality and risk deep dive (15 min)
3. Clarification loop performance (10 min)
4. Asset/governance updates (10 min)
5. Improvement plan approvals (15 min)

## KPI snapshot template
- On-time delivery (OTD%)
- Average turnaround time (TAT)
- QA pass rate
- Open risk count
- Clarifications per 1k words

## Commentary prompts
- What improved vs last month and why?
- Where did AI acceleration help most?
- Where did human intervention prevent business risk?
- Which bottleneck should be removed next?

## Improvement plan template

| Focus Area | Current Gap | Proposed Action | Owner | Due Date | Success Metric |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Terminology | Frequent term overrides | Tighten approval SLA | PM + Linguist | YYYY-MM-DD | +2pt term adherence |
| QA latency | Late major issue discovery | Shift-left sampling | QA lead | YYYY-MM-DD | -20% late defects |
| Clarifications | Blocker spikes | Pre-briefing pack update | Client owner | YYYY-MM-DD | -30% blocker rate |

## Executive summary format
- 3 wins
- 3 risks
- 3 commitments for next cycle
